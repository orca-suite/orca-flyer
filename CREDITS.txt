ORCA FLYER
Credits & Data Sources
======================

ORCA Flyer integrates publicly available data, imagery, audio, and open-source
components to support educational visualization and research.

DATA SOURCES
------------
Satellite Orbital Data:
  CelesTrak (https://celestrak.org)
  Provides publicly accessible orbital element sets for research,
  education, and non-operational use.

Planetary Constants & Ephemerides:
  NASA/JPL NAIF SPICE Toolkit
  https://naif.jpl.nasa.gov

Earth Imagery:
  NASA Visible Earth
  https://visibleearth.nasa.gov

Country Flag Images:
  Derived from public-domain national flags obtained via Flagpedia.net
  Used solely for identification and educational purposes.
